Lewis. You can't analyze away predication in general in terms of universals. This is because in order to analyze predication, you need predication. So, there is an infinite regress if you are trying to get rid of the predication or simplify it. THe new theory is to pick out the natural properties.

Definition. A property is a set of things. But does equivelance of members mean equivelance of properties? Lewis doesn't have this problem because he takes his sets over all the possible worlds. But there's a problem with this, because there is no set of everything.
